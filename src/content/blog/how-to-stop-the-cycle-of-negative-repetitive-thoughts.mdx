## üî≤ Box Breathing Technique (5 in - 5 hold - 5 out - 5 hold)

**#1:** Breathe in slowly while counting to 5.

**#2:** Hold your breath for 5 seconds.

**#3:** Breathe out while counting to 5.

**#4:** Hold your breath for 5 seconds.

Repeat.
