#### This is the process of the most effective self-hypnosis exercise I know

#### STEP 1:

1.  **Name 5 things you see out loud:** _"I see a closet. I see a carpet," etc._

3.  **Name 5 things you feel out loud:** _"I feel warmth in my legs. I feel the wind blowing over my left arm," etc._

*   4x see, 4x hear, 4x feel
*   3x see, 3x hear, 3x feel
*   2x see, 2x hear, 2x feel
*   1x see, 1x hear, 1x feel
