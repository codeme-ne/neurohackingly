### ü§ì Studying

[Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc/) - The Pomodoro technique is a powerful technique where you concentrate for 25 minutes on a task, followed by a 5 minutes break. It lets you track your progress with planting one tree per "pom session".

[Wingwave](https://wingwave.com/wingwave-app/) - positive melodies, mainly due to a left-right beat, decrease stress and increase creativity and concentration. [How it works.](https://wingwave-shop.com/en/pages/so-wirkt-wingwave-musik)

[Breathe](https://achim.io/breathe) - My favorite app for guided breathing exercises.
