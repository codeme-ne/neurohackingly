## After logging some sessions, analyze trends by asking chatGPT.

I asked ChatGPT about focuslog trends and patterns. It shared valuable insights:

*   Best focus times: 11am - 1pm and 3pm - 5pm.
*   Breaking admin work into 15-minute chunks.
*   Taking regular breaks.
