DESIGN RATIONALE:
Diagonal split creates visual tension and energy, breaking the typical grid.
Content-heavy left side with conversational copy, visual elements on right.
Stats/social proof integrated naturally. This layout says "different thinking."

KEY CSS CONCEPTS:
- CSS `clip-path: polygon()` for diagonal split background
- Two-column grid with overlapping diagonal visual element
- Photo positioned with transform/translate for dynamic feel
- Animated gradient background on one side (subtle neural blue sweep)
- Stats cards with hover effects

PROS:
✓ Visually distinctive and memorable
✓ Energy and movement without being chaotic
✓ More conversational, personable copy space
✓ Social proof integrated organically

CONS:
✗ Diagonal elements can feel trendy/dated quickly
✗ Complex responsive behavior (diagonal must adjust gracefully)
✗ Accessibility: ensure sufficient color contrast across split
✗ May be too dynamic for some professional contexts
