DESIGN RATIONALE:
Magazine-style layout with large hero image on left (40% width) and content
card on right (60%). The photo becomes a statement piece, not just decoration.
Latest posts preview provides immediate value and encourages scroll.

KEY CSS CONCEPTS:
- Grid: `grid-template-columns: 2fr 3fr` on desktop
- Photo: full-bleed with `object-fit: cover`, slight grayscale filter
- Content card: subtle background (--bg-elevated), padding: 3rem
- Typography: larger sizes, editorial leading (line-height: 1.7)
- Optional: parallax scroll effect on photo

PROS:
✓ High-impact visual presence (photo is hero element)
✓ Professional, authoritative aesthetic
✓ Content preview adds value immediately
✓ Leverages horizontal space well

CONS:
✗ Photo quality becomes critical (needs professional headshot)
✗ More complex responsive breakpoints
✗ Risk of feeling corporate/impersonal if not executed well
✗ Large image may slow initial load
